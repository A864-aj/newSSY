What is SSY ?

SSY is the fundamental knowledge of life. Our ancient rishis had formulated a unique mode of training called “Brahmopadesam”, which is an instruction on the science of non-doing and experiencing stillness within and abundance without .

It brings about a sea change in one’s outlook towards life and effects remarkable maturity in the individual. SSY is the present-day version of this ancient science of “Brahmopadesam”.
